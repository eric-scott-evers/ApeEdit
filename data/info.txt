                                                                                                                   
  ApeEdit is an experimental editor.                                                                                                                                   
                                                                                               
  It is built from Erlang and Elixir.                                                                                
  You can also build coworkers(plugins) from any beam language                                                                                
  like lfe, or gleam, etc.                                                                                 
  For instance, each menu is created by a different Elixir process.                                                                                
  If one menu crashes, the other menus are uneffected.                                                                                 
                                                                                                         
  Many processes help the editor for function,                                                                                
  some of the named processes are listed.                                                                                                                  
                                                                                                      
    char_queue_for_tether,                                                                                
    ignore_data_queue,                                                                                
    build_event,                                                                                
    listen_char,                                                                                                                                        
    cecho_srv,                                                                                 
    menu_event_queue,                                                                                
    char_queue,
